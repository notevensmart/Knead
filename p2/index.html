About Knead

Mental health care that makes room for context.

Knead provides accessible mental health services delivered by therapists and mental health professionals.

The practice helps clients ease into understanding mental health as a complex network of emotions shaped by the individual, the community, and the wider world.

Knead recognises the contexts that affect mental health and the flexibility people need as they move through care.

Siri Indukuri

Siri is passionate about promoting awareness of mental health and wellbeing across all age groups. She has worked in the field for the past 10 years with people of diverse ages and backgrounds and is a strong advocate of everyone having equal access to mental health care.

Siri has particularly worked with adolescents, teenagers and young adults and recognises the impact of the ever changing expectations of these age groups.
