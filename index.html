About

A softer starting point for mental health support.

Knead helps clients ease into understanding mental health as a complex network of emotions shaped by personal experience, relationships, community, and the wider world.

The practice recognises that mental health is contextual, flexible, and deserving of care that feels human, respectful, and easy to move toward.
