# Extracted Requirements - Knead Website

Business Name:
Knead

Business Description:
Knead provides accessible mental health services delivered by therapists and mental health professionals. The business helps clients ease into understanding mental health as a complex network of emotions between the individual, the community, and the wider world. Knead recognises the contexts that affect mental health and its flexibility.

Website Goal:
Provide a simple starting point for users to discover the mental health services offered, pricing or cost information, and how to get in touch.

Target Audience:
People aged 18-40 from diverse backgrounds.
